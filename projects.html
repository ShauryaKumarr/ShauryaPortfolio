My Projects

Undergraduate AI Researcher @ UD

Engineered a sophisticated medical AI tool that transforms user-inputted information into accurate patient vignettes. This tool leverages the Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) technique to effectively mitigate bias in large language models (LLMs). I also implemented advanced evaluation techniques for text summarization performed by Transformers, incorporating BLEU and ROUGE metrics to assess the correlation between patient context and the generated vignettes.

RoomieUD (Hackathon Winning Project @ HenHacks)

A Tinder-inspired app that uses Euclidean geometry algorithms and numerical point-based questionnaires to find compatible roommates with incredibly accurate results.

Sensor Developer/Researcher @ DSU

Funded by DoD & NSF. Utilized Python and a Raspberry-Pi to configure an OPC-N3 optical sensor to output PM readings with user-editable features.

SurgiScan (1st Place @ HopHacks, Patient Safety Technology Category)

A YOLOv5-powered application that leverages computer vision to accurately detect and track surgical tools in real-time during procedures, preventing them from being left inside the body.

Wall Of Support

(Still in early stages of development, works better on computer. Click info icon on website for full project statement.)

An educational project turned into a meaningful one by creating a public forum where people all around the world can receive and send words of encouragement and positivity (please check it out! Leave a comment or two :))

Sentinel Aid

An AI-driven disaster management system that leverages drones to reach inaccessible areas during emergencies. Features include S.O.S. requests, automated footage analysis, and a community-driven aid exchange platform for disaster victims.
