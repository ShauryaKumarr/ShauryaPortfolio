Undergraduate AI Researcher @ UD

Engineered a sophisticated medical AI tool that transforms user-inputted information into accurate patient vignettes. This tool leverages the Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) technique to effectively mitigate bias in large language models (LLMs). I also implemented advanced evaluation techniques for text summarization performed by Transformers, incorporating BLEU and ROUGE metrics to assess the correlation between patient context and the generated vignettes.
